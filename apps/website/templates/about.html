About Us

Learn more about Homa Bay Concrete Products Manufacturers Ltd and our commitment to quality and sustainability.

Company Overview

Homa Bay Concrete Products Manufacturers Ltd (HBCP) has been at the forefront of concrete manufacturing in Kenya. Established with a mission to provide top-quality concrete products for sustainable infrastructure development, we have built a reputation for excellence and reliability in the construction industry.

Mission: To provide top-quality concrete products for sustainable infrastructure development.

Vision: To lead in innovation and customer satisfaction in Kenya's construction industry.

Our Technology

At HBCP, we utilize state-of-the-art technology in our manufacturing processes. Our production line features the advanced StoneMaker DM100 machines, which are designed to produce high-quality concrete products efficiently. Coupled with imported molds, our technology ensures precision and consistency in every product we offer.

We are committed to continuous improvement and innovation, ensuring that our products meet the highest industry standards and customer expectations.
